CIBIL Score

It is important to have a CIBIL score of 750 plus to get attractive rate of interest on your Home loan. CIBIL data indicates that 80% of the home loan approvals are given to customer who have a credit score of 750 plus. Low CIBIL score could possibly reject your home loan application or you may have to pay a higher interest rate.

Additional Funds

A couple of banks have a facility, which allows borrowers to park their additional funds in the loan accounts. This will reduce the interest proportionately from the principal amount for the time that the amount was parked. This is an interesting option.

Bridge Funding

Every borrower has to pay some money from his own pocket while buying a house. Try to pay as much as possible as down payment. This will reduce your interest paid on the principal.

Floating Rate and Fixed Rate

When it comes to taking a loan, the first thing that will come to your mind is the interest rate. There are two forms of interest rates—fixed and floating. The fixed rate is one that is not affected by market fluctuations, whereas floating rate changes accordingly. The fixed rate is slightly higher than the floating rate.
