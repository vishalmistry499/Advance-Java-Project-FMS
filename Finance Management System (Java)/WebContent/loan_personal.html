CIBIL Score

It is important to have a CIBIL score of 750 plus to get attractive rate of interest on your Home loan. CIBIL data indicates that 80% of the home loan approvals are given to customer who have a credit score of 750 plus. Low CIBIL score could possibly reject your home loan application or you may have to pay a higher interest rate.

Take Personal Loan only in case of emeregency

It is advisable to opt for a loan only when you are facing a financial emergency. Loans come with interest cost and should not be taken for discretionary expenses. Non-payment of a single EMI may hurt your credit score making it difficult for you to get other loans in future.

Consider your expenses and choose right amount

Based on the prices and expenses associated with your needs, you can decide the amount that you need to borrow. If your monetary needs are unpredictable, consider opting for a flexi personal loan.
